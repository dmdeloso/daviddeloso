Journalist and Designer

I’m a second-year studying journalism and human-centered design at Northwestern University. More importantly, I'm a multimedia storyteller with experience in photography, audio, video, graphic design and web design. I enjoy using these skills to tell stories focused on people.

Beyond journalism, I’m interested in exploring media innovation through human-computer interaction. I'm always looking for ways to go beyond the scope of traditional forms of storytelling and create new experiences for readers. Let’s push the boundaries together!
